Formula: WBGT = 0.7·Twb + 0.2·Tglobe + 0.1·Tdry (outdoor, with solar load). Natural wet-bulb and globe temperatures are estimated from air temperature, RH, wind, and solar/surface input using standard meteorological approximations (Stull 2011 for Twb; a simplified radiative globe-temp model for Tg) rather than direct sensor measurement, so treat results as estimates, not certified industrial-hygiene readings. For occupational exposure decisions, use a calibrated WBGT meter and your jurisdiction's official heat-stress guidance.
